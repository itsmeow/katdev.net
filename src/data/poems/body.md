her body rests among

you oh how it rings

true and though we've

folded our last hearts

through and i will never

give up on you

---

you are worth it to

live and breathe all

that you can do

it is within you

---

there is only one

truth it's belief in

the self and in Earth

she is nothing without you

---

to hug an ant and provide

a shelter and food for

the self and mind

you will have to

forgive yourself and

nobody else can save you

just you.

how the arm folds onto the fingers and paints a picture of something alive you must see

look at her and look at me there is something flowing in your veins

feel the air pushing you

into this endless room in which your eyes become one with her and you can see it

hold your body or you will surely lose the sensation,

your body is one single unit and run, run, run and imagine how it feels to be you

as you are living and you are here

do not let go of it.

and remember that everyone can read this and feel alike- how can anyone take this away from you?
